This manual is to help you configure extension Incentives
<br/><br/>

1. Enable extension and choose where incentives will apply (in the cart or checkout process)
<br/>
2. Go to menu Extensions -> Order Totals and enable Incentive Total
<br/>
3. Go to menu Sales → Incentives and create new.
<br/>
4. After creating you can set conditions of incentive and bonuses.
<br/>
5. To check how it works go to storefront and look at subtotal block on the cart/checkout page.
